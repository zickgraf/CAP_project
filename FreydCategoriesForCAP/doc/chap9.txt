  [1X9.4-9 UnzipDegreeList[101X
  
  [33X[1;0Y[29X[2XUnzipDegreeList[102X( [3XS[103X ) [32X operation[133X
  [6XReturns:[106X  [33X[0;10Ya list[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YGiven  a graded row or column [3XS[103X, the degrees are stored in compact form. For
  example, the degrees [ 1, 1, 1, 1 ] #! is stored internally as [ 1, 4 ]. The
  second  argument  is thus the multipicity with which three degree 1 appears.
  Still,  it can be useful at times to also go in the opposite direction, i.e.
  to  take  the compact form [ #! 1, 4 ] and turn it into [ 1, 1, 1, 1 ]. This
  is  performed  by this operation and the obtained extended degree #! list is
  returned.[133X
