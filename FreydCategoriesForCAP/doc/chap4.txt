  [33X[0;0YLet [23XA[123X be an additive category. The Adelman category of [23XA[123X is the free abelian
  category  induced by [23XA[123X. An object [23Xx[123X of the Adelman category of [23XA[123X consists of
  a  composable pair [23X(\rho: a \rightarrow b, \gamma: b \rightarrow c)[123X in [23XA[123X. We

  [33X[0;0YGiven two objects [23Xx = (\rho: a \rightarrow b, \gamma: b \rightarrow c)[123X and [23Xy

  from  [23Xx[123X  to  [23Xy[123X  in the Adelman category of [23XA[123X consists of a morphism [23X\beta: b
  \rightarrow  b'[123X,  called  the  [13Xmorphism  datum[113X,  that  has  to fit into some
  commutative  diagram  of  the  form  Any  such  morphism  [23X\omega[123X is called a
  [13Xrelation witness[113X, any such morphism [23X\psi[123X is called a [13Xcorelation witness[113X. Two
  morphisms  between [23Xx[123X and [23Xy[123X with morphism data [23X\beta[123X and [23X\beta'[123X are congruent
  iff  there  exists [23X\sigma_1: b \rightarrow a'[123X and [23X\sigma_2: c \rightarrow b'[123X
  such  that [23X\beta - \beta' = \sigma_1 \cdot \rho' + \gamma \cdot \sigma_2[123X. We
